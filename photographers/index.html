Ottavia Brussino

Impact Photographer & Founder
Impact professional who has worked with international organizations, social ventures, governments and NGOs across Europe, Africa and Central Asia. She built a passion for photography during her travels and work, recognizing the power of authentic visual storytelling to amplify the voices of communities creating change. Currently at the Paris branch of J-PAL, MIT-based impact evaluation center, she founded Scatto to address the critical need for ethical storytelling in the impact ecosystem.

Specialties

Impact evaluation, international development, community engagement, policy innovation

Regions

Europe, Africa, Central Asia
